Astrology & Horoscope Systems

Astrology is a powerful science that helps understand life patterns, karmic influences, and future possibilities. Below are the key astrological systems used for accurate guidance.

3.1 Kundli (Birth Chart)

Kundli, also known as Janam Kundli or Birth Chart, is the foundation of Vedic Astrology. It is prepared using date, time, and place of birth and represents the exact planetary positions at the moment of birth.

A detailed Kundli analysis helps in understanding personality traits, career prospects, marriage compatibility, health patterns, financial growth, and important life events. Accurate predictions depend on precise birth details and proper interpretation.

Kundli is not only used for predictions but also for identifying planetary strengths, weaknesses, doshas, and suitable remedies to balance life challenges.

3.2 Horoscope

Horoscope predictions are based on planetary movements and their influence on the twelve zodiac signs. These insights help understand trends related to health, career, finance, relationships, education, and personal growth. Horoscope readings should be used as guidance to make informed and balanced life decisions.

Horoscope predictions are generally calculated using Moon Sign for higher accuracy. Individual results may vary depending on personal birth details and planetary combinations.
